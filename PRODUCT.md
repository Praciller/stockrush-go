# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Technical recruiters and engineering reviewers evaluating a portfolio project in under ten minutes. They need to understand the concurrency claim, reproduce it locally, and inspect the implementation evidence without learning a custom interface.

## Product Purpose

Make the zero-oversell proof legible and runnable: 1,000 buyers compete for 100 items, exactly 100 reservations succeed, inventory never becomes negative, and retries or workers never duplicate effects.

## Brand Personality

Rigorous, direct, calm. The interface should feel like a trustworthy engineering evidence notebook, not a sales dashboard.

## Anti-references

Generic admin templates, dark neon observability screens, crypto styling, glass panels, trophy walls, decorative charts, and any live-looking status that is actually static evidence.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with reconciled evidence, not claims.
2. Keep every demo action bounded and reversible.
3. Label live and pre-generated states honestly.
4. Let standard controls and tables disappear into the review task.
5. Explain the database boundary where correctness is won.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA: keyboard-operable controls, visible focus, semantic landmarks and tables, status text that does not rely on color, reduced-motion support, and readable contrast at mobile and desktop widths.
